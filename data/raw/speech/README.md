# data/raw/speech

Raw audio clips representing **non-violent speech** (talking/voices, including singing/humming).

## Current subfolders
- `conversation/` — natural multi-person conversations.
- `murmur/` — quiet/mumbled speech, low-energy talking.
- `phone_call/` — speech recorded during/over phone calls.
- `scripted/` — scripted/read speech (audiobook-like, monologue).
- `singing_humming/` — singing and humming.
